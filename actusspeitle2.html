Butterflies are usually deep and powerful presentations of life and hope. The butterfly signifies the changes and alterations in your life wherein every time you see a butterfly, you ask yourself what changes you need to live more in alignment with your authentic truth. Also, butterflies appear to remind us in any circumstance that things are going to be okay. They also are known for welcoming new hopes and positivity even you are encountering obscurities and difficulties. It represents life struggles that most people endured to developed as a more reliable person. A butterfly is indeed a symbolic creature that delivers lessons concerning life. Hope is also one thing that the butterfly means. The butterfly in sunrise color also signifies hope.

Concerning our situation right now, wherein we encounter hardships because of the pandemic, like this little creature, who makes a way in faith, accepts changes, emerges from transitions gracefully, we should understand that worries and frustrations are useless against constant changes in nature. Thus, whatever uncertainties we are going through, we shouldn't lose hope because hope keeps us recommence our journey in life.
