A pandemic is defined as the “worldwide spread of a new disease”. The pandemic had caused our daily lives to drastically change. It started on December last year and it was declared a global pandemic on March 11, 2020. The virus came from a city in China called Wuhan. It was a respiratory illness that spread though out China and eventually all over the world. The world has approximately 113 million cases of Covid 19. There are also 63. Million recovered and 2.5 million deaths. Currently the nation that has the greatest number of Covid 19 cases is the United States of America with 28.5 million cases. Here in the Philippines, we have a total of 571k cases of Covid 19. There are 525k recovered and 12,247 deaths.



The impact of the COVID-19 pandemic is drastically changing the lives of people, including the lives of young people. Schools and universities have closed momentarily, exams and events postponed, the usual health information services are limited, socializing with friends and wider family is highly discouraged and in some places even punishable. Living in these circumstances can be tough for young people for their social, physical and mental wellbeing. This study examined the prevalence of psychiatric symptoms and identified the factors contributing to psychological impact in the Philippines. During the early phase of the pandemic in the Philippines, one-fourth of respondents reported moderate-to-severe anxiety and one-sixth reported moderate-to-severe depression and psychological impact. The factors identified can be used to devise effective psychological support strategies.

Due to the global pandemic, the schools are forced to shut and cancel all face-to-face classes and meeting as much as possible to prevent the widespread of the virus. Over 1.2 billion students in 186 countries are also forced to stop going to school. Because of this, all education sectors came up with another way to let students learn inside the safety and comforts of their home and this is the e-learning system or the usage of digital and electronic devices to connect with the class as a medium of education as a new normal life it adopted. There are also apps that students are able to connect with the teacher and their other classmates like google meet, zoom, and existing apps like messenger and facebook. In this medium students use cellphones, laptops with the apps to comply to their activities, quizzes and exams. Another way is through modular learning, this is for the students that don’t have the capacity to have the e-learning system. They are given booklets to comply their tasks.

Though the pandemic is continually affecting the world and the lives of everyone, we can prevent this virus from harming us, we should follow the health protocols that the government has proposed. One of the ways to get infected by the virus is between direct contact, indirect or close contact with infected people through respiratory droplets. The virus can infect people of all ages, however people who are low in immunity has a higher risk to get severe Cpvid 19. Older people who are over 60 years old has also a higher risk of getting the virus. The ways on how to prevent the virus from infecting you is to wear masks. Avoid going to crowded places and stay at home as much as possible. Also keep yourself clean and sanitized. You can also get the vaccine for Covid 19 to make sure that you are safe.
